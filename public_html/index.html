About me

Ryan Becker is an aspiring web developer from Albuquerque, New Mexico. After spending nearly ten years working for a family business, he decided to make a change and get into the tech realm. Ryan enjoys meeting people and making new connections. He is also passionate about technology and how it can simplify life and solve problems. When he’s not coding he can be found spending time with his family and two dogs. He is also an avid video gamer and hopes to someday get involved with the development of top tier games. Ryan is originally from Chicago but has lived in Albuquerque since 95’. He enjoys eating pizza and the great outdoors, namely snowboarding and hiking.
